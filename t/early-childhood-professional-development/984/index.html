Early childhood professional development

Christy

We are planning an early childhood professional development workshop for non early childhood educators. This course has given us some new ways to help the participants "be a young child." We would like to have them do hands on activities as a 3, 4, 5,or 6 year old given the developmental parameters of those ages.
