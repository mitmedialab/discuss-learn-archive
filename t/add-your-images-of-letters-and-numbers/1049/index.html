Add your images of letters and numbers

James_B

Creatives of all flavors, I am trying to develop a simple and fun signal tester for those reprogramming their MaKey MaKey for the first time.

This is a Scratch project that randomly selects images from collections and displays them when a user pushes and holds keyboard keys.

I need more images! Everything will be welcome that is respectful of the Scratch community and is pretty, funny, clever, quirky, zany, personal, or a mix of these things. I am especially looking for topics that are relevant to MaKey MaKey use like conductive nouns.

If you would like to help:

  1. See my project and click "See Inside" to begin remixing.
  2. Click the first sprite titled "ADD HERE!"
  3. Add your costumes! They can be for A-Z, 0-9, the arrow keys, or the spacebar!

Try pressing and holding A, B, or C while in my project to see some of what I've submitted to our collection!
