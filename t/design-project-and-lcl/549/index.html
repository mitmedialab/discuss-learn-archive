Design project and LCL

danishbuddha

As part of my master study we are doing a design project which is gonna be showed at the IDC2014 conference here in Denmark in june.

our first edition vision:
Can design thinking and design processes help educators bring imagination into educational systems?

Our goal is to share our experiences, findings, results, ideas etc. relating to educational design and theory. With these we will try to bridge the gap between playing and learning. Our common ground is a shared Master of Arts (Education) in ICT-based Educational Design.

I'm the only one of the group doing the LCL course do to the others work schedule, but we all hope that we both can get a lot of input and share ideas from this course. I did LCL1 last year and i know how much input its possible to get from this course, even through its not the same syllabus.

We gonna be blogging about our project at www.legoglaering.dk and i'll try to keep this thread updated also.
