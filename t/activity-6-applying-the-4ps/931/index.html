  by  timtak 

Last week, something pretty great happened at work for one of my colleagues. I won't bore you with the details, but let's just say that she reached a huge milestone.

When it happened, it was late in the day, and most folks had already left. However, we were both so excited by her success that there was whooping and hollering amid the empty space. Then there were selfies with our "good luck cat" (see image above).

All in all, it was downright silly. We certainly could have shaken hands and smiled. However, our sense of play got the best of us. As we laughed and laughed, I realized that we all need play and celebration in our lives.

When something good happens, celebrate it. Be silly. Dance. All in all, it will make your work and your relationships more fulfilling.

As I reach the end of the Learning Creative Learning Course with MIT, we had to think of a way to embody the principles we've tried in the course. For me, it's the "Good Luck Cat" badge. Every time I want to play and celebrate, I'm breaking it out!


How do you play?
