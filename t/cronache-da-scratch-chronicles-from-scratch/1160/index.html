Cronache da Scratch, (Chronicles from Scratch)

CyberParra

Cronache da Scratch, (Chronicles from Scratch) is a book (self-published and written by @raffaella and me) that contains seven short funny stories about Scratch world as seen by the sprite's side. It tells stories of how sprites live inside Scratch cloud, what they feel about coders, and how they relate with code applied to them. So, a funny way to talk about Scratch, coding and creative computing.
