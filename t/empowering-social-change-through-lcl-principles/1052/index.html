Empowering Social Change through LCL Principles

shari

Cynthia de Windt and I decided for our LCL2 Project to articulate our philosophy in a clearer and more deliberate manner, showing how we pulled together the LCL 4P's and our own 3H's into a cohesive creative learning strategy to then build our Social Change learning events on. This is only one part of that documentation, but it's hopefully an easy way to visually understand our thought process from LCL1 to LCL2. We are currently working on a more complete text draft documenting our approach and all the inspiration and motivation we draw from to create these innovative learning environments, so stay tuned...!

For anyone interested, we use a software called Mindjet to create our mind maps.
