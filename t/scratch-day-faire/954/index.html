Scratch day faire!

lgarmon

Blake and I invite you to join our project to put the 4 Ps into Practice: We are meeting on Monday at 10 am in Room 445 to brainstorm about how to make Scratch Day this year look more like a carnival/fair. There are a half dozen activity areas, and the idea for the event is to make it like a fair experience, where you can wander from one activity area to the next, with no set workshop times.
Using that as our inspiration, we are going to have some fun introducing some visual elements to fortify the "fair/carnival spirit."

If you are local to Cambridge (or thereabouts), and you are good with wood working, fabric manipulation, set decorations, posters, party decorations, or anything along these lines, please join our project. The more the merrier. If you'd like to join this project, but you cannot meet on Monday, feel free to contact me. Post here or contact me directly at:
