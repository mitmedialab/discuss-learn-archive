How do you say ABC in your language?

  1. go to this Scratch studio
    http://scratch.mit.edu/studios/408640/
  2. remix one of the project
  3. go to the sounds menu
  4. select one letter, Using the sound editor delete the current sounds
    and record your voice
  5. complete the alphabet
  6. rename your file adding the language "Say ABC xxxx"
  7. add your project to the studio
