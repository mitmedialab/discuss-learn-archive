LCL & Design Alternative Education Program: PERMIE KIDs

Jen

Hi,

I'm Jen Mendez. The Learning Creative Learning (LCL) has positively impacted how I've taken my ideas for PERMIE KIDs and put them into practice that will hopefully inspire creative learning for educators and children alike.

I made a short video about how I'm applying what I've learned in LCL to not only my life, but to an alternative educational program called PERMIE KIDs - http://www.permiekids.com. This is an educational design that seeks to empower educators (in the broadest sense of the word) and children through a passion-driven, project-based educational program designed with children, not just for them. If you are interested, you can check out the video at https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SBToy5kpsuo.

I want to thank not only the instructors, but all the other learners as well. This course has been helpful to take my ideas and design a process that puts the 4Ps into practice. I received some great feedback and resources from the breakout panel today.

I look forward to any questions or comments. If anyone wants to chat in the future, you can email me at jen@permiekids.com.

Cheers,
Jen
