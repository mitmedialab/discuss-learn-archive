New category for Week 1 - Making it easier to find things

1L2P

In order to make it easier to navigate the discussion forum and find the information you are looking for, we changed some of the category names and moved a few topics around. The links we sent out should all still be working - if you notice any strange behavior, or links are not working anymore, please let us know (just reply to this post).

Here is what changed:

There is now a category called "Week 1 - Creative Learning" that aggregates all topics related to the first week of the course, including links to the videos, the activity of the week, and the readings.

The category also contains a number of discussion topics created by you (like this topic by the Oliver fanclub). Feel free to create more topics relevant to the ideas in week 1.

We intend to have a category for each topic of the course. They will be named Week 1 ... 6, but it doesn't mean you have to access them in order. Or that the discussions in category Week 1 should stop as soon as Week 2 starts. This is all probably just getting more confusing, so I'll stop here.

You'll find all the relevant topics for this week here > http://discuss-learn.media.mit.edu/category/week-1-creative-learning
