Hi, there!

My name is Tiffany, and I'm a PhD student in the Lifelong Kindergarten Group at the MIT Media Lab. I work on a platform called Build in Progress which is designed to help people share the story of their design process.

We're launching a Mystery Messages-themed Challenge on Monday, June 9th, which is open for anyone to participate in. Throughout the challenge, participants will create design projects while sharing how their project evolves, getting feedback from one another, and getting inspired by what others are creating.

You can find more information about the challenge on the following page:

http://buildinprogress.media.mit.edu/mystery_messages_challenge

Hope some of you are able to join us!
