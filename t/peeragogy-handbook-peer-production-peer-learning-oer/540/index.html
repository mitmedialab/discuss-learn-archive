Peeragogy is a collection of techniques for collaborative learning and collaborative work. By implementing effective patterns for working together, we hope to leave the world in a better state than it was when we arrived. Over the last couple of years a number of peers have come together to create a handbook on Peeragogy for other peers interested in Peer Learning and Peer Production. We wrote the book collaboratively online and in the process learned more about peers can co-produce knowledge.

We would love to have the brilliant people involved in this course take a look at it and let us know what you think! Please get in touch with us via our Google+ Community (search Peeragogy in Action) on Twitter @Peeragogy and/or by e-mailing me c@danoff.org
