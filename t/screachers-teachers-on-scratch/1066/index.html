SCREACHERS! Teachers on SCRATCH

Sandy

This project, SCREACHERS (Teachers on Scratch), was started last year as part of CCOW. Here is the promo project, studio and companion website.

The Screachers Studio is an area in Scratch specifically dedicated for mentoring. The studio is used as a way to reach out to the students of the Scratch community. The mission is to encourage young learner to go further, to foster their creativity, to provide them with resources and help, and to ultimately keep them engaged in creating.
