The Chronicles of Scratch - crowdfunding campaign

Hallo All
Maybe some of you remember I wrote The Chronicles of Scratch, seven short amazing stories about the Scratch's world seen by the sprite's side.

The Chronicles of Scratch is currently in italian, but is the time to make an English version. Don't you agree?
To do that, Chronicles of Scratch is live on kickstarter.

The funds will be used to pay the translation from italian to english, allowing us to create an ebook in english version, to be published on Amazon and on other online publishers.
If you like the project, and/or if you would read the ebook in english, you might consider to be a backer and to share the campaign with your contacts
Help us to create the english version of The Chronicles of Scratch
Thank you all
